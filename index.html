About Me

I grew up primarily in the South and Midwest. Spending my early years in this region gave me an affinity for nature, agriculture, and the outdoors. My exposure to nature has also made me an animal lover. In fact, I'm a proud cat mom and squirrel whisperer. Traveling is also one of my other favorite pastimes. When traveling, some of my favorite places to visit are where I can immerse myself in different cultures, find unique science attractions, and enjoy good cuisine.
